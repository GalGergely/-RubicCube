About

The Old Pochmann method is a popular method for solving the Rubik's Cube, which combines memorization and two-step algorithms.

The goal of the Old Pochmann method is to solve the cube using simple, easy-to-remember moves that swap corner and edge pieces one at a time into their correct positions.

Neccesary iformation

  • The "play" button opens the auto-solving mode of the cube.
  • The "algorithm mode" button opens the leanrning mode. You can add your own algorithms, or learn the pre programmed ones, if you dont need them anymore, you can delete them later.
  • The "settings" button opens a new page, where you can set the color of the cube, and some other settings. You can make the cube stop in every move, or make the face-ids show.
  • The "info" button will open this webpage.
  • Your keypresses won't register until the animation is over. For the flowless working of the program and for the best experience, please always wait for the animation.
  • You can "ruin" the solving by:
    • Turning the cube while it solves itself.
    • Turning w or m moves, when you shuffle the cube. (clockwhise and counter clockwhise both)
